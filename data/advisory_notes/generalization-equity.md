SYNTHETIC SAMPLE — not real clinical guidance.

The board's most consistent methodological concern is external validity. AI
lung-nodule tools are often validated on a narrow set of scanner vendors, slice
thicknesses, and reconstruction kernels, and on demographically skewed cohorts.
The board wants the gap analysis to check whether performance is reported across
scanner manufacturers, dose levels (screening low-dose vs diagnostic), slice
thickness, and patient demographics, and to flag single-site or single-vendor
validation as a generalisation risk. Post-market monitoring for performance
drift across sites was raised as an expectation that predicate labeling rarely
addresses.
