topic: Subsolid and part-solid nodule performance
---

SYNTHETIC SAMPLE — not real clinical guidance.

Advisory board members with thoracic subspecialty training repeatedly note that
subsolid (ground-glass and part-solid) nodules are where CAD/AI tools are
weakest and where the clinical stakes for indolent adenocarcinoma are highest.
Detection and, especially, growth characterisation of part-solid nodules is
harder than for solid nodules, yet most vendor validation cohorts are
solid-nodule-heavy. The board wants any gap analysis to check whether the
literature and the predicate labeling report performance *stratified by nodule
type*, and to treat "performance on subsolid nodules unreported" as a
first-order evidence gap rather than a footnote.
