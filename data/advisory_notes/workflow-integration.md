SYNTHETIC SAMPLE — not real clinical guidance.

Beyond raw accuracy, the board treats workflow integration as make-or-break for
adoption. A triage device that promises reduced time-to-report only delivers
value if it integrates with the reading worklist and PACS without adding clicks.
The board is skeptical of turnaround-time and "worklist prioritisation" claims
that were measured in a research setting rather than in a live reading room, and
wants any unmet-needs analysis to separate *demonstrated in deployment* claims
from *demonstrated in a retrospective dataset* claims. Prior-CT comparison
(automatic registration of the current study against priors for growth tracking)
was called out as a high-value, frequently-absent capability.
